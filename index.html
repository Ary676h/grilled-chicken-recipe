This juicy grilled chicken is easy and perfect for any meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Mix olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Rub mixture over both sides of chicken.
  4. Grill 6â€“8 minutes per side until fully cooked.
  5. Let rest 5 minutes, then serve.
